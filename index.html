Abalone Age Predictor

This is a Jupyter Notebook for analyzing and creating a regression model to predict the age of an abalone using Python. I made use of many of the standard Data Science libraries, including Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib/Seaborn, Sci-Kit Learn. The regression model performed very well on dataset with less than 14 rings (RMSE ~1.5), which is the majority of the data. There was an RMSE of ~4.5 for the abalone with at least 14 rings.

BodyFat Prediction

This project was focused on predicting bodyfat percentages based on measurements. It is very hard to get an accurate prediction with the naked eye. Cheap methods (using calipers) are very unreliable and accurate methods are very expensive. By perform extensive feature selection and model selection, an ElasticNet model yielded the best and simplest results, which had a mean absolute error of ~3.5%.
